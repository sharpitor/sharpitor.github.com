Detailed Description

Rib manifold manager (model-view)

The 'haplo' idea is to borrow from the analogy with an evolving population of of haploid indiviudals.

Thus interactive editing of the rib manifold proceeds by division, culling and homogenization variegation (mutation) subject to collective constraints
